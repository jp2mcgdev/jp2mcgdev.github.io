JP II Men's Group

When:
​

Our men's group meets every third Monday of the month, unless otherwise stated.
Its a great way for men to come together in solidarity to live the life of a Catholic Gentleman.
Please consider joining us!

Participation in The Sacramental Life of the Church:
​
At every meeting, the men in attendance have the invaluable opportunity to participate in the sacramental life of the Church through the recourse to the sacraments of Reconciliation and the Eucharist.

Curriculum: 
​

While event discussion topics currently vary from month to month, generally we are working through Jason Evert's book "John Paul the Great." By reflecting upon the life for our group's patron, we share striking insights and discuss how to apply lessons and virtues of the man sat at the heart of Church for a quarter centry.

Fraternity
Every month, we offer the opportunity for men to come together and share in a supportive brotherhood that will help strengthen them for the journey ahead by the active participation in the Body of Christ. 

Take an active role in our discussions. Receive competent and trustworthy answers to your questions from our monthly priest (who often stays for the discussion) as we explore our assigned curriculum together. 

Hospitality 

Every men's group we offer a wide variety of delicious food, libations, and desserts. Our menu ranges from fish boils to pot roasts. Don't miss out on the opportunity to come together as men and eat a home cooked meal. 

One of our annual family events consists of a delicious cookout and bonfire with live music. This normally takes place during the first weekend of September. Don't miss out on the chance to live in solidarity with other members of the Body of Christ.
