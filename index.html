The Mission

To strive for continual catechesis among our Catholic brethren by utilizing the tools given to us by Holy Mother Church. As a faithful brotherhood united in our devotion to the Blessed Sacrament, we learn and go forth to catechize those who have gone astray from the fullness of Truth, in our vocation to bring souls to Christ.


The Trailer

"Feed My Sheep" (John 17:21)
Central to The New Evanglization is going forth and engaging the culture using new methods and means. To simplify coordination for such events, We operate "The Mobile Sacristy" Trailer which contains most items require for celebrating reverent liturgies.

Men's Group

"It is not Good that Man is Alone" (Gen 2:18)
The irony of an increasingly virtually connected world is the proportional rates for reported loneliness. We meet monthly to share in person fellowship, prayer, and laughter to review this communal wellspring.
